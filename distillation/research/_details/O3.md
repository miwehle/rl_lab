## Interpretation Notes

The score deltas are small compared with ordinary landing-score variance and have mixed signs across worlds. This is evidence against a strong inference-time dependence on `dv_x/dv_y` in the finished greedy policies.

The action-agreement and Q-delta columns show that the networks still read these inputs. The result is therefore not "`dv_x/dv_y` are unused"; it is "`dv_x/dv_y` are used, but other cues can often compensate in greedy inference."

This fits the HPO history in [[../../../hpo/research/observations#O10 Five-World 10D Reaches 253|HPO O10]]: the velocity deltas may have improved trainability and the search landscape even if the final policies do not strongly require them at inference time.
